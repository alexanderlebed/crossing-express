Operating Principles

1. Structural Clarity over Optimism. We model jurisdictional structures as they are, not as they are marketed.

2. Sovereign Responsibility. Final implementation decisions remain with the individual. We do not replace legal counsel.

3. Controlled Opacity. SRIM methodology is structured and internally coherent. Certain modeling layers remain undisclosed.

4. No Emotional Leverage. We do not operate on fear, urgency, or political agitation.

5. Continuous Calibration. The model evolves as regulatory environments shift.

Crossing operates as a strategic navigation laboratory, not an advisory agency.
