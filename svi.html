Sovereign Volatility Index (SVI)

The Sovereign Volatility Index is a structured expert-model framework developed by Crossing Research Division to evaluate jurisdictional volatility across mobility-relevant structural vectors.

Conceptual Foundation

SVI measures structural volatility, not desirability. It evaluates frequency of change, severity of impact and policy predictability across five systemic vectors.

Methodological Structure

SVI compresses five analytical vectors:

• Regulatory Acceleration • Migration Policy Stability • Capital Transparency Exposure • Tax Regime Fluidity • Geopolitical Discontinuity Risk

Each vector is assessed using a structured F / S / P model.
