Charter of Analytical Neutrality

Crossing operates as an independent analytical modeling structure. Its function is structural synthesis — not advisory promotion.

I. Non-Affiliation

Crossing maintains no financial, political or institutional affiliation with jurisdictions evaluated within its modeling framework. No country, regulator or migration pathway is promoted.

II. Absence of Commission Structures

Crossing receives no referral commissions, no country-based incentives and no relocation brokerage compensation. Modeling outputs are not influenced by commercial partnerships.

III. Analytical Sovereignty

All SRIM outputs are structured under internal analytical standards. External narratives, political positioning and media cycles do not dictate modeling logic.

IV. Separation from Legal Representation

Crossing does not provide legal filing, immigration submission or tax structuring services. Implementation remains external to the modeling institution.

Analytical neutrality is not a marketing position. It is a structural requirement for long-horizon sovereign modeling.
