Strategic Interpretation

Portugal remains one of the most stable mobility jurisdictions within the European Union. While fiscal pressure has increased after the restructuring of non-habitual resident programs, the jurisdiction continues to provide relatively accessible residency pathways and low geopolitical exposure. The primary long-term volatility drivers remain regulatory harmonization within the EU and increasing fiscal transparency requirements.
