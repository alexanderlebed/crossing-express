Strategic Advisory Council

Crossing operates as a modeling laboratory within a rapidly evolving jurisdictional environment. To preserve structural coherence and intellectual integrity, its strategic direction is informed by a council framework.

The Council is not a public-facing body. It represents a conceptual layer of oversight aligning mobility modeling with long-term geopolitical and regulatory dynamics.

Its role is to ensure that SRIM remains structurally neutral, analytically disciplined and institutionally consistent.

Crossing does not operate as a consultancy. It operates as a sovereign modeling structure guided by long-horizon analytical discipline.

Institutional form precedes institutional scale.
