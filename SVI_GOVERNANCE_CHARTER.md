# Sovereign Volatility Index (SVI)
## Governance Charter v1.0

---

## 1. Purpose

This Charter defines the institutional governance framework of the Sovereign Volatility Index (SVI).

SVI is a structured expert-model analytical instrument maintained by Crossing Strategic Navigation Laboratory.

Its purpose is to provide disciplined jurisdictional volatility mapping across mobility-relevant structural vectors.

---

## 2. Institutional Position

SVI is:

- Not a rating agency
- Not an investment advisory product
- Not a political index
- Not predictive

SVI is an analytical compression framework designed for sovereign positioning analysis.

---

## 3. Governance Authority

The SVI framework is maintained under the authority of:

Crossing Strategic Navigation Laboratory

Methodological revisions require:

- Version increment
- Public protocol update
- Documentation of structural changes

---

## 4. Versioning Policy

SVI follows semantic structural versioning:

Major Version (e.g. 2.0)
→ Structural methodology change

Minor Version (e.g. 1.2)
→ Weighting refinement or scoring clarification

Patch Version (e.g. 1.1.1)
→ Language clarification or documentation update

All historical versions remain archived.

---

## 5. Data Assessment Framework

Country assessments:

- Are expert-structured evaluations
- Are time-bound snapshots
- Must include:
  - Assessment date
  - Version number
  - Vector-level F/S/P breakdown
  - Narrative context

SVI does not auto-update.

Revisions must be explicitly published.

---

## 6. Update Cycle

SVI assessments may be:

- Annual
- Event-driven (major geopolitical or regulatory discontinuity)
- Methodology-triggered

Each update must include:

- Revision note
- Change explanation
- Version reference

---

## 7. Analytical Integrity Principles

SVI operates under the following principles:

- Structural neutrality
- No affiliate incentives
- No commission-based influence
- No political advocacy
- No jurisdictional promotion bias

SVI evaluates volatility, not desirability.

---

## 8. Licensing Framework (Future Provision)

SVI may be:

- Licensed for institutional analytical use
- Integrated into advisory platforms
- Referenced within research publications

Licensing terms shall preserve:

- Methodological integrity
- Attribution requirements
- Non-manipulation clauses

---

## 9. Institutional Disclaimer

SVI is a structured expert index.
It does not forecast events.
It does not guarantee outcomes.
It maps structural exposure at time of evaluation.
